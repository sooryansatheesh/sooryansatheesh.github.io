About this project

Tripura Forest Department operates large number of timber depots where timber of different kinds obtained through scientific harvesting or through seizure from illegal smuggling. The sale of timber from this stock is a significant source of revenue for the organization. There was difficulty in knowing the real time stock position of timber of different species of trees spread across different depots. Lack of timely disposal results in damage to timber and loss of revenue. Local people also didnâ€™t know the availability of timber of different species. In order to solve these and other pain points, this information system was developed.

My Role (Deputy Conservator of Forests (IT & Statistics) in the Tripura Forest Department)

As the product manager on the product, I carried out the following:

  1. User interviews with officials at different levels of management and local people to understand their requirements
  2. Translate user requirements into EPICs
  3. Conducting user research for understanding the requirement better.
  4. Prioritizing product features for subsequent versions
  5. Reviewing all design work and integrating and engineering.
