About this project

Agar Tree is a crucial raw material used in the perfume industry and Tripura stateâ€™s environmental condition promotes growth of the highest quality of this product. Farmers cultivating these trees however require permission from the Tripura Forest Department for cutting the trees and selling them. The delay in issuing permissions for cutting trees was cited a major pain point by the tree farmers due which they are discouraged. To resolve this road block the government decided to allow self certification of such plantations by the farmers and to develop a mobile app to help farmers easily do such self certification. The project was executed by the NTFP Centre of Excellence under the Tripura Forest Department.

My Role (Deputy Conservator of Forests (IT & Statistics) in the Tripura Forest Department)

In this project I provided :

  1. Detailed guidance and consultation to the Director of NTFP Centre of Excellence regarding mobile app/software development.
  2. Prepared detailed workflow for User Registration, Plot Registration, Tree Information Entry and Tree felling process consulting the relevant rules and regulations and user interviews with responsible government officers
