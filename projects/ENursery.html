About this project

Tree plantation projects for climate change mitigation requires large numbers of planting material in the form of seedlings raised in plastic polybags in controlled forest nurseries and which when mature are transported to remote locations for planting. The department produces on an average a million seedlings of different species in over 30 forest nurseries across the state. The local people also visit the nurseries for purchasing tree saplings, which is good source of revenue for the organization.

There was difficulty in knowing the real time stock position of seedlings of different species of trees spread across different nurseries.
