Current Focus

This District 4 Vegetation Monitor project exemplifies my commitment to leveraging geospatial technology for environmental analysis and decision-making. By combining satellite imagery analysis, advanced GIS processing, and interactive web visualization, this project demonstrates how data-driven approaches can provide valuable insights into environmental changes and support informed resource management decisions.

The project integrates multiple geospatial datasets and processing workflows to create an accessible, interactive platform that enables stakeholders to understand and monitor vegetation dynamics across California's transportation corridors.
